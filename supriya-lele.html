The Brief

As part of a live industry brief at UAL London College of Fashion, I developed a full 360° communication campaign for Supriya Lele's AW21 collection. The campaign needed to launch the collection to a global audience, grow the brand's social media presence, and establish Supriya Lele as a name in international fashion press.

Supriya Lele is a British-Indian womenswear designer whose work explores her dual cultural identity through materiality, cut, and fabrication. Her AW21 collection drew on early 2000s nostalgia, Indian colour palettes, and tribal tattoo prints: playful, confident, and post-pandemic in its optimism.

My campaign strategy centred on three pillars: a launch film, a brand collaboration with Glossier, and an immersive Y2K-themed launch event at Sketch in Mayfair. I designed the lookbook mockups and the photo-realistic event concept visuals, and wrote the press release targeting British Vogue, Dazed, Cosmopolitan, and i-D.

The Designer

Strategy

Campaign Strategy

The campaign was structured around three core objectives: growing Supriya Lele's Instagram following by 5–10% in launch week, reaching 50,000 views on the launch film, and securing coverage from Cosmopolitan Magazine. The strategy also targeted a sell-out of the Supriya Lele x Glossier collaboration.

Campaign proposal: structure, timeline, and objectives

Campaign Aims

  • Grow Instagram followers 5–10% in launch week
  • 50,000 views on launch film, featuring Lila Moss
  • Supriya Lele x Glossier collaboration to sell out
  • Secure Cosmopolitan Magazine coverage

Target Press

British Vogue Dazed Cosmopolitan i-D

Press

Press Release

I wrote the full press release for the AW21 launch, targeting fashion journalists and editors across four key publications. The release covered the collection narrative, the Glossier collaboration, and the launch event, framed around the Y2K cultural moment Supriya Lele was responding to.

Press release for immediate release, 18 September 2021. Written and designed by Estelle Ankrah.

Gifting

PR Gifting Box & Collaboration

I conceptualised a PR gifting box designed to immerse press and influencer recipients in the Y2K world of the collection. Each box contained a custom invite delivered on a flip phone, the Supriya Lele x Glossier limited edition collaboration pieces, Y2K sunglasses, lookbook polaroids, and themed confectionery. The Glossier collaboration (limited edition mascara and concealer in custom holographic packaging) was designed to create a beauty bridge between the brand's existing audience and Supriya Lele's world.

PR gifting box contents: flip phone RSVP, Glossier collab, Y2K glasses, lookbook and popcorn

Supriya Lele x Glossier limited edition collaboration, holographic packaging

Estelle's Work

Lookbook Design

I designed the campaign lookbook mockups, placing AW21 collection pieces into a Y2K-saturated visual world of hot pink interior spaces, neon lighting, and maximalist styling that channelled the 90s/2000s aesthetic at the collection's heart. The lookbook was designed to function as both a press asset and a digital social piece.

Lookbook design. Design Mockup: Estelle Ankrah (2021). Supriya Lele AW21 collection styled in Y2K-themed set design.

Estelle's Work

Event Concept

I designed the photo-realistic concept visuals for the AW21 launch event, set in the Pink Gallery room at Sketch in Mayfair. The event was conceived as a prequel to the launch film, following a group of friends getting ready for a night out, set in a maximalist Y2K world with cosmopolitans, french fries, edible glitter, and the Supriya Lele x Glossier launch.

Photo-realistic event design concept, Estelle Ankrah (2021). Launch at Sketch, Mayfair.
