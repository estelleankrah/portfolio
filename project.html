Press Week
at John Lewis

Brand & PR

Overview

In July 2024, I represented John Lewis at Press Week, one of the retail calendar's most significant media moments. My role sat at the intersection of PR operations, stakeholder liaison, and brand communications, precisely the kind of cross-functional work I had trained for at UAL.

I managed direct relationships with journalists attending the event, coordinating access, briefings, and follow-up correspondence. Alongside this I oversaw the influencer gifting programme: curating selections, preparing documentation, and ensuring every touchpoint reflected the John Lewis brand standard.

A key output was a competitor analysis report, tracking how rival retailers were positioning themselves across press and social channels during the same window. This fed directly into our post-event strategy review.

Approach

Preparation began weeks before the event. I built and maintained a media contact database, tracking journalist briefs, outlet priorities, and relationship history. Every gifting parcel was logged with recipient details and product compliance records, a discipline that came directly from my work as a JL Finance and Insurance Representative.

During the event itself, I balanced reactive media requests with proactive content creation for press social channels. I briefed internal teams, managed supplier communications, and escalated issues through the correct channels quickly and clearly.

John Lewis & Partners

Disciplines

Outcome

Delivered full press and influencer programme across a five-day event window with zero briefing errors.
