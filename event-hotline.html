The Placement

In 2022, I completed my placement year with Event Hotline Rentals (EHR), a black-owned Ghana-based agency established in 2005 with a focus on PR, events, and luxury. Based in Accra on Amar Kwarteng Street, EHR operates across two divisions: Event Hotline (the main agency) and Event Hotline Rentals (a rental supplies business serving the events industry). The agency donates a percentage of its annual proceeds to developing villages in Ghana.

My role was Social Media Manager Assistant, brought in as part of a broader rebrand effort. EHR wanted to reposition themselves as a contemporary agency to expand their client portfolio and reflect the digitalization the agency had undergone. I was one of three interns and was the only intern working remotely from the UK at the start of the placement.

The placement gave me my first experience of real international agency life, managing multiple concurrent tasks, working to tight deadlines, adapting to a different professional culture, and using creative software skills I had developed at UAL in a live commercial context.

The Agency

Event Hotline Rentals

EHR has hosted major events for Ghanaian socialites across all occasions, and in recent years diversified into PR events, including work for Larry Jay Ghana, one of Ghana's most established fashion brands. The agency's dual structure (event management and equipment rental) made it a distinctive environment to work in: operations, client relationships, and creative communications all ran simultaneously.

EHR office on Amar Kwarteng Street, Accra. The showroom displayed luxury event hire items including glassware, tableware, and furniture for rental.

The Brief

Estelle's Work

Rebrand: Official Logo to New Identity

EHR's existing brand was built around their iconic shield and unicorn logo: a mark that communicated luxury, heritage, and ambition. My placement brief was to help evolve the brand toward a more contemporary identity that could reach a wider, digitally-native audience. I designed the new EHR logo: an orange Africa silhouette encircled within a globe, using Monument font for a futuristic feel. The colour palette (orange for excitement, black for sophistication) and the Africa motif reflected the agency's ambition to influence events across the continent.

Official logo: the Event Hotline unicorn shield. Luxury, heritage, ambition.

Estelle's rebrand: orange Africa/EHR mark. Futuristic, pan-African, digitally-native.

The Role

Estelle's Work

Social Media Manager Assistant

My core task was to assist EHR's rebrand, from their existing identity to a more contemporary aesthetic aligned with their growing PR and digital presence. I organised their event boxes and client calendar, mocked up logo design concepts, created and managed social media content, and handled business messages across their platforms. I used Adobe Creative Suite (developed through UAL workshops) to create and edit content, and introduced Planoly to the team for scheduling, which allowed me to auto-post and take on more work simultaneously.

Daily to-do list: sort itinerary, pack event bags, review brand documents, email clients, review engagement analytics on Instagram

Accra, Ghana, where the placement was based. Travelling to Ghana for the in-person element of the role.

Impact

Estelle's Work

Social Media Growth

Over the three-month monitoring period I grew and managed EHR's Instagram presence. Posts had been dated before my arrival so I recreated a new aesthetic that matched the rebrand direction. Using Planoly for scheduling freed time to engage with followers and manage the content calendar. Engagement grew substantially across the period, with reach extending beyond Accra to London, Tema, and Kumasi.

Instagram analytics over 90 days: 1,613 followers · 68 accounts engaged · 108 content interactions · Top locations: Accra (63.8%), London, Tema, Kumasi

Client Work

Tilly Hair GH Event

In week two I took on one of the most challenging tasks of the placement: packing over 600 event gifting boxes for Tilly Hair GH, a hair vitamin brand client, within four hours alongside two other interns. I then drafted and sent personalised emails to 48 guests and influencers confirming their boxes, asking them to use the hashtag #TillyHairGH when sharing content. I completed the task before the deadline and credited the experience with clarifying my professional goals: I thrive in fast-paced, creative and dynamic environments.

The email I drafted and sent to guests and influencers for the Tilly Hair GH event, personalised using the brand voice from their Instagram. 48+ recipients.
